Module 01 · Foundations

Course Module Outline

By the end of this module, learners will be able to:

  • Identify the core principles of AI-augmented learning design.
  • Distinguish between generative and analytical AI use cases in L&D.
  • Map human-in-the-loop touchpoints across a course workflow.

This module is structured around three short activities:

  • Concept map — pair-share the boundary between AI assistance and authorship.
  • Prompt teardown — analyse a failed prompt against the intent-outcome gap.
  • Workflow audit — locate one process in your team to instrument with AI.

Submit the following before the live session:

  • A short reflection (200 words) on your team's prompt-to-output loop.
  • A screenshot of one workflow you intend to instrument with AI.
  • One question you'd like tackled in the live walkthrough.

End the module with a 60-second written answer to:

  • What part of the workflow will remain human-driven, and why?
  • Where does the AI get the final say, and where do you override it?

Keep your answer in your private learning journal — it will be referenced in module 02.
